Nuke Comments

Situation Room

Are you getting comments on Google+ from people that you don't want commenting on your posts? Time to go nuclear on them! Hit the Nuke button and delete the comment — Nuke Comments will then block them so you'll never be bothered by their annoying comments again.

Extreme Measures

HYDROGEN MODE: One click nuking.

Use at your own risk!

In Hydrogen Mode clicking the Nuke button immediately
deletes the comment AND blocks the person AND reports for spam.

Privacy Policy

I care deeply about your privacy. This extension uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as counting clicks on the Nuke button, and tracking the number of people blocked using this tool. This information will not be used to identify users, and will only be used to identify usage trends.
