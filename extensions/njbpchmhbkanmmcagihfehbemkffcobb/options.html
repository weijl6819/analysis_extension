What is felt temperature?

Felt temperature is the temperature you perceived outdoor. It is different from simple air temperature because the effect of relative humidity and wind speed are considered.

In humid conditions, the air feels hotter than it actually is, because of the reduction of perspiration.

In windy conditions, the air feels cooler than it actually is, because of increased perspiration.


How is felt temperature derived?

This extension calculate felt temperature based on the following references:

  • Heat index (Wikipedia)
  • North American wind chill index (Wikipedia)
  • If neither the criteria of heat index nor of wind chill index are satisified by the parameters, plain air temperature is displayed.
