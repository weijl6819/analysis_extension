  • Open TabToDo extension to see all open tabs
  • Turn an open tab into a Task by clicking the + button
  • Rename the task you just created. Turn it into an action item
  • Once completed- mark the task as done by hovering over it and ticking the checkbox
  • Use the visual indicator to get an immediate tab/task status
  • Click the X button to close a tab/task
  • Have control over closed tasks, as well as tasks that were not completed. You can always reopen any closed tab
